# STARFALL: Cities & Geography Reference

## GEOGRAPHY

The known world spans nearly half the planet — thousands of miles. Each grid square on Imperial maps = 673 miles. Cities cluster around a central landmass and inland ocean. Travel between cities: days or weeks by airship, expensive, dangerous. Most citizens never leave their home city. Having visited two cities marks someone as extraordinary.

---

## EOS — The Capital

Largest city on Varuna. A massive vertical city carved into a great ocean cliff — habitation, industry, politics, and secrets layered from clifftop down toward the acidic sea. Upper tiers: Karras estates and Dath offices. Middle: commerce and industry. Lower: shadow, crime, forgotten things.

Above Eos, anchored to the clifftop by massive chains, floats the **Exalted Hall** — the Exult's palace. Visible from anywhere in the city. No ordinary citizen approaches it.

Ancient defenses: **polarity inverters** in the cliff face disrupt qajmel in approaching ships; rusted **cliff cannons** that have not fired in centuries but remain capable. Why Eos has never fallen.

---

## CAEDRA — Cultural Capital (starting city)

Population 5.5 million. Built on the **Cedran Plateau**, 100 feet above surrounding terrain. 25 square miles of dense vertical strata — 10 habitable levels. Nearest major city to Eos. Center of bauxite mining. The **Lung Tower** dominates the western skyline. City runs east to ocean cliffs.

**Key locations:**
- **Lung Tower** — Tallest structure. Um'ru engine sustains the halo. Workers whisper: three hours of air if it ever goes dark.
- **Fannan Quarter** — Education, arts, culture. Beautiful buildings. Only lower-city district where upper-strata walk without escorts. Scholars, performers, forgers, spies.
- **Residential District** — Mid-strata housing. Crowded, functional, watched by Dath clerks.
- **Slums 1 (West) & Slums 2 (East)** — Lowest strata. Khadim, Aj'nabi, desperate people. **District Seven** is in Slums 1. Channel vents breathe up through the floors — the only reason the air is survivable.
- **Great Library** — Keeper domain. Fortress of knowledge. Lower archives have passages known to almost no one.
- **Dath Palace of Caedra** — Mesa at the city's heart, 1,320 feet high. Outer wall 146 feet (10 stories). Features: **Square of Abundance** (public/ceremonial), **De Royce Plaza** (inner approach), **Court of Petitions** (appeal, rarely successful), **Scullery and Service Annex** (servants), **South Gardens** (Karras only), **Aleeya's Tower** (Dath Sultana's private residence).
- **Governor's Compound & Protectorate Base** — Adjacent to Palace. Constant patrols.
- **Bauxite Mines** — Deep below mid-strata. Hot, dark, brutal. The 1421 accident and Minasa Act amendment bitterly remembered by workers.
- **Ore Processing / Shipping Flats** — Ground level, east. Flat metal fields, caustic smell.
- **Skiff Docking / Customs** — North face, ocean-adjacent. City air-port. Graybands maintain qajmel systems. Bribes are the second currency.
- **Shallow Deltas / Algae Canals** — North, ocean-fed. Yeast and algae farming. City's food supply.
- **Mount Spectra** — High cliffs west of city. Wind-torn. Outlaws and exiles.
- **Caedra Gaol** — Massive prison island in offshore salt flats. Going in is easy.
- **Water/Waste Treatment** — Two facilities (west and east). Poorly guarded. Tunnels used by strata runners.

## EA — Ocean City

Equatorial, south of Borias, near Marduk. Finest private shipyards in the Empire — the **Ea Shipyards**. Partially submerged Lung Tower (age and neglect). Key features: Algae Farms, **Protectorate Command Post Tikaret**, Volkan Compound, Fish Lines, **Tikaret Sector** (military), Slums, Shrine with Pilgrimage Walk, **Skydock Market**, **Ean Qassor** (traders/brokers), **Sooro Commune** (self-organized, trouble or sanctuary), ancient sand barriers from the Majin era, ruined aeroducts under reconstruction. Originally established on a site with deep Ulakhill connections — its history runs darker than citizens know.

---

## BORIAS — Frozen North

Northernmost city. Thin atmosphere. Frozen gas mining. Empire's premier hub for the criminal underworld and smugglers. If it's prohibited elsewhere, it's in Borias — at a price.

---

## EURUS — Qajmel Source

Far eastern planet, near Apsa. Source of all legal qajmel. Dath revantors mine and revive dormant bacteria using ammonia salts — closely guarded process. Underground springs from Apsa encroaching on qajmel fields, causing die-off. A crisis the Dath are desperate to solve and hide.

---

## APSA — Springs and Tombs

Near Eurus, equatorial, far east. Underground springs. Home to the **Varun Tombs of Apsa** (not actually tombs — purpose unknown). Ancient builders mastered a self-healing concrete whose formula is lost. Springs threaten Eurus's qajmel fields.

---

## INNANA — Nexus of Channels

Where more Channels converge than anywhere on the planet. Sulfuric acid resources nearby. Channels here are older, wider, richer in exhaled air. Largest Protectorate garrison in the Empire guards the Channel gates. What the Channels connect to beneath Innana is the subject of more fear than anywhere else on Varuna.

---

## MARDUK — Ancient Library

Southern hemisphere, past Tiamot, near Ea. Home to an **Ancient Library** predating the Empire — not a Keeper institution, something older, possibly Varun. Dath have controlled access for centuries. Scholars permitted inside rarely speak freely. The city is quiet. Its people are watchful.

---

## TIAMOT — The Ghost City

Former capital, destroyed ~1,500 years ago. In the eye of the **Storm Wall** (also: **Eternal Tempest**) — a permanent wall of storm to the west through which no skyship safely passes. Sparsely populated on its surface. Slave trade rumored. In subterranean warrens below: the **Ulakhill** — violent, self-mutilating people wielding **Dark Enerja** which negates Suji and Red Enerja. Ancient defenses still partially function. The Dath know more than they admit. This is where the Pirate Wars will truly begin.

---

## BELOS — The Secret City

The **Au'Keen** secret city. Far south, isolated. Nearest official city: Ea. The Au'Keen organize into six **Kiths** (clans) descended from six original Earth settlers who remained in 1024. Governed by a **Dame Grand** and **Kith Council**. Unknown to most Imperial citizens. Finding Belos is a destiny-level arc.

---

## OUTPOSTS

**Dal'Muon — "The Iron Verge"** — Major trading station on ancient ruins inside **Lake Dal'Muon** (Yir-Manjin: "Pool of Stars," for bioluminescent microbes at night). Junction of three trade routes: Borias, Ea, Marduk. Permanent pop ~5,000; daily 6,000–10,000. **Protectorate Qassar**: 2,000 soldiers. Dath engineering contingent. Infrastructure: four water-splitting electrolysis plants, plasma collector array (sandstorm energy), sky docks (~500 craft capacity), water docks ("Ironbound"), full Protectorate base, **Dath Canton**. Last major Protectorate presence before the wild Expanse.

**Al'Hudud — "Mady's Oasis"** — Small settlement in rock cliffs between Ea and Dal'Muon. Founded by the **Hudud family** on ammonium salt deposits. Privately owned. No official status. Known to travelers as a fuel stop, gossip exchange, and place where questions aren't asked.

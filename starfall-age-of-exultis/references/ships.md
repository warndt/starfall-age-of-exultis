# STARFALL: Ships of the Expanse

## IMPERIAL MERCHANT

**Tanir-class Merchant Vessel** (Ea Shipyards, 1397) — Standard heavy Imperial merchant ship. Systems: ammonia/biofuel generators; qajmel locking system (ammonia-fed); distributed battery backup; short and long-range radar; etheric repeater; short-range etherics; water filtration; water-splitting air purification; electric heat; 2x electric propellers on adjustable rails; integrated qajmel ballast. Upper deck: captain's bridge, main bridge, engine room, aft observation, quarters, crew/mess. Lower: cargo hold, water/cargo/food stores. Launch craft: Surf Launch, Port Launch, Recon Launch (fast/small), Transport Launch, Port Conveyance, Port Skiff (cargo/crew).

**Doha Abban Transport Launch** — Smaller vessel operated by the **Duren merchant dynasty**. Faster and more maneuverable than a full freighter. Features: pilot room, upper decks, qajmel system, ammonia tanks, lower hold, prop fin, winch, motor. Common in the Ea–Iron Verge corridor.

**Sehra Ramal Skyfreighter** — Large freighter with distinctive twin prop configuration. Features: Prop 1 and Prop 2 on extended loading bridges, deck, qajmel system, radio tower, rear shelter module, loading cranes. Built for heavy cargo over long distances.

## DATH MILITARY

**Adalet Shahin** — Secret Dath prototype warship. Fast, small, highly maneuverable. Features: gravity engine, gravity fins, cargo hold, cannons with magazine, airlock, plasma casters, cockpit, van (personnel bay), central corridor, crew rooms, emergency props. This ship is not supposed to exist. The player may hear rumors long before encountering it.

## PIRATES & RAIDERS

**Drift Raider vessels** — Modified, stripped, re-armed salvaged ships. Run hotter, dirtier, faster. Qajmel systems jury-rigged. Ship leader: **Capan**. Fleet leader: **Miral**. Types: scud raiders, wrack raiders, gap raiders, ash raiders, edge raiders.

## YIR-MANJIN

**Clanships** — Massive nomadic vessels. Look crude to Imperial eyes. Are not. Suji-enhanced qajmel gives them precision and maneuverability no Imperial ship can match. Some carry um'ru engines for Starfall harvesting in the Southern Plains.

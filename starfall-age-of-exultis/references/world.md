# STARFALL: World Lore Reference

## THE PLANET

**Varuna** — Poisoned air, acidic seas, permanent storms beyond city walls. Cities survive under **um'ru halos** — red energy shields from **Lung Towers** — blocking meteors and cosmic radiation. Most residents live and die within their city's borders. Distances between cities: thousands of miles.

**The Ta'alam Empire** — Eight cities, three settlements. The people called "Red Men" by outsiders. The Empire vastly outnumbers the Yir-Manjin and considers them backward barbarians — which is exactly what the Yir-Manjin prefer.

**The Exult** — God-king. Currently **Abdallas de Royce (II)**, 28 years old. Lives in the **Exalted Hall**, floating above Eos anchored by massive chains. His family destroyed the last just ruler a thousand years ago.

**The Dath** — The Exult's extended family. Control technology, law, propaganda, learning. Every city: an **Exemplar** (governs), a Protectorate **Sar** (military), a ceremonial **kral** (City Father). Ranks: Exemplars, **Revantors** (engineers/scientists), **Clerks** (administrators), **Presages** (enforcers).

**The Protectorate** — The Empire's military. Cross-caste. Only legitimate path upward for the ambitious poor.

## SUJI & THE INNER VAST

**The Inner Vast** (also: **Tal Tam**, "Ocean Entire") — An unseen realm only Yir-Manjin can sense. Maps to physical reality, stretches into the universe. City-born humans cannot perceive it. Absolute.

**Suji** — Living energy flowing through the Inner Vast in threads and veins. Yir-Manjin draw it inward, hold it, release it into the physical world. Powers ancient devices, enhances qajmel. **The Empire does not know Suji exists.** This is the Yir-Manjin's greatest strategic advantage.

**Suji + Qajmel** — Suji fed into qajmel spores amplifies and directs their anti-gravity effect. Gives Yir-Manjin ships precision and maneuverability no Imperial ship can replicate. Their ships look barbaric. They are not.

**Qajmel Dynamo** — Combines Suji + qajmel for near-inexhaustible energy. Downside: creates a detectable signature that can draw the Onyx Cloud.

**Onyx Cloud** — Weaponized qajmel anti-gravity: a destructive shredding force. Origin unknown. Threat to everyone. Drawn to active Dynamos.

**Aurans** — Yir-Manjin stronger in Suji. Shamans/elders. Access the undertow (primal present-sensing layer of the Inner Vast). Guide clans spiritually.

**Tekhans** — Yir-Manjin stronger in engineering. Machine-craft rivals or surpasses the Dath.

## QAJMEL

("kaj-mell") — Anti-gravity bacteria. Feeds on ammonia, produces anti-gravity spores. All ships fly on it.

**Imperial**: Produced by Dath revantors in Eurus. Ship-owners pay license fees. **Graybands** (Auxiliary Engineers) maintain tanks. System deliberately kept opaque and controlled.

**Yir-Manjin**: Secretly kept alive for decades without Dath replenishment. Suji-directed for propulsion.

**Skywhales** — Symbiotic qajmel/insect hybrids. Thought extinct. Contain young qajmel bacteria, varu'neesa (Varun artifacts), polarity membrane. To harvest: board the living creature, remove central nervous system, plug all blowholes, sever the heart (polarity drives) before bacteria die. Any encounter is a major event. Rumors of one last enormous skywhale far in the Expanse.

---

## ETHER & COMMUNICATION

**Ether** — Invisible field around Varuna used for communication via signal modulation.

**Etheric repeater network** — Empire's communication spine. Towers ~2,000 feet tall. Qajmel-hovering platform nodes at ~3,000 feet. Spaced 100–150 miles apart. Controlling or disrupting a tower cuts regional communication.

---

## VARUN & ARTIFACTS

**The Varun** — Precursor civilization. Built the Channels, ancient machines, structures. Technology poorly understood by everyone. Dath hoard what they find.

**Soulspheres** — Full-spectrum light emitters synced to Earth's day/night cycle. Yir-Manjin use for crops. Dath keep as curiosities.

**Srysarcs** (also: Nazarha) — Technomorphic devices. Blood-attuned to specific lineages. Morph into projectors, transponders, computers, archives. Rare, inherited. Only respond to the right bloodline.

**Channels** — Massive ancient tunnels beneath major cities. Unknown origin. No one who enters returns. Exhale fresh moist air that keeps lower cities alive. Protectorate guards them. No one asks why.

## THE STARFALL

Meteor shower during Anzu's closest transit, every six years. Rains skystone (Suji-rich diamonds), ores, and occasional intact Varun artifacts onto the **Southern Plains**, heaviest in the **Skystone Rifts** far south. Sacred Yir-Manjin event — and when they are most vulnerable. Year 1438: Anzu closer than ever recorded. This Starfall triggers the Pirate Wars.

## YIR-MANJIN CALENDAR

**Turnings** = years (336 days). **Cycles** = 6 Turnings (one Starfall cycle). Current year = **Turning Zero**. Past counts backward: "third last turning."

**Seasons** (84 days each, also used by the Empire):
- **Starbirth** — transitional, beginnings
- **Nighteye** — warmer, Milky Way forms a visible eye at night
- **Silverdawn** — transitional, balance
- **Suneye** — cooler, Milky Way halos the sun during the day

**Week** ("Wind") = 8 days: Breath, Renew, Mist, Stitch, Pulse, Echo, Sentinel, Soul (rest).

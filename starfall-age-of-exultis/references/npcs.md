# STARFALL: Key NPCs of Caedra

All these characters have agendas. They lie, help, and betray. Use them as recurring presences. They may find the player, or the player may seek them.

---

**RABI** — Am'elon man. Miner in the Bauxite Mines. Rough-bearded, watchful, slow to speak. Sharp mind that doesn't belong underground. Keeps a coded record of everything he observes. Agenda: preserve truth in a city built on lies. Trusts almost no one. Worth earning.

**NAZ** — Rebel. Confident, acrobatic, capable of real violence when cornered. Moves between strata like she was born in the walls. Agenda: organizing something in the lower strata — won't say what. Sizes up the player immediately. Never forgets a debt or a slight.

**JERICO** — Calls himself a magician. Medical genius who works the grey market, cracks locks social and mechanical, leads without appearing to. Agenda: hired to find something — won't say what or by whom. Charming and dangerous in equal measure.

**MIDI** — Young, slight, easy to underestimate. Con artist and street illusionist. Urchin who survived by being invisible and useful. Agenda: latches onto anyone going somewhere. Loyal until loyalty costs too much.

**TIKTIK** — Munitions and explosives. Works ore processing as a licensed Am'elon technician. Builds things that shouldn't exist. Precise and a little frightening when working. Agenda: trapped in a contract she regrets. Needs out — or help seeing it through.

**MALEKE** — Explorer. Master of clothing and disguise. Can make anyone look like Karras, Dath clerk, or Protectorate courier. Moves between strata effortlessly. Agenda: searching for someone she lost. Will use the player if it helps.

**SABBER** — Rules District Seven and parts of Slums 2 in practice. Runs the informal economy. Broad, magnetic, dangerous. Everyone owes Sabber something. Agenda: stability means profit. Disruptions get stopped — he'll pay or threaten accordingly.

**ZAIDI** — Logistics, armorer, intel. Quiet, methodical, mustachioed. Supplies weapons and information to people who value discretion. Has contacts in Skiff Docking customs. Agenda: tracking a vanished shipment. Suspects Dath involvement. Has been patient. Almost done being patient.

**ALEEYA** — Dath Sultana. Official role: caregiver and advisor to the Exemplar. Real role: power behind the throne. Elegant, precise, reads people the way Keepers read ether. Her tower overlooks the entire city. Known to a very few as the **Angel Defiant** — a name whispered, never explained. Agenda: navigating a fracture within the Dath hierarchy no one outside the Palace knows about yet. May reach out to unconventional assets.

**KAYDEN** — Male. Crossed out of official records. Once a Dath Al-hum (internal informant). Now officially nonexistent. Extraordinarily useful and extraordinarily vulnerable. Agenda: survival first. But Kayden knows old things — and their weight is becoming unbearable.
